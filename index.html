Latest trend

This week we will focus on a growing french events promoter & record label from Hossegor in France : BINARYSOUND

Straight from the Landes region, the Binarysound collective has been participating for 5 years in the development of the electronic scene in the south west of France. For the past few months, the collective has also been active on the Parisian scene. About me

My name is Alexandre Chevallier, I'm a 25 year old french Dj and music lover. I have been Djing for 4 years and a member of the BinarySound crew for 3 years. I live in Bordeaux but I was raised in Hossegor, where my music adventure began.
